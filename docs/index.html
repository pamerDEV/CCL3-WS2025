App Concept

MyBuddy is a 4-in-1 wellbeing tracking app featuring a virtual companion called Buddy. Users can track their mood, habits, sleep, and water intake. Buddy reacts emotionally through expressions and animations based on the user’s wellbeing.

A hidden Wellbeing Score (0–100) is calculated from all tracked data. Instead of showing numbers, Buddy reflects the score through mood and behavior, creating emotional engagement and motivation.

Goals & Features

  • Mood tracking with 8 emotional states and optional notes
  • Habit creation and streak tracking (CRUD)
  • Sleep logging with duration and quality ratings
  • Water intake tracking with customizable daily goals
  • Animated Buddy reacting to the Wellbeing Score
  • Full customization of Buddy (name & color)

Wellbeing Score

The Wellbeing Score combines four dimensions: mood, habits, sleep, and water intake. The score is calculated internally and never shown directly to the user. Instead, Buddy’s mood reflects the current state.

Component Max Points Calculation
Mood 30 Based on logged mood type
Habits 30 (completed ÷ total) × 30
Sleep 25 Duration + Quality
Water 15 (intake ÷ goal) × 15

Buddy Mood States

  • EXCITED (85–100): Bouncing animation with sparkles
  • HAPPY (65–84): Gentle breathing with a smile
  • WORRIED (45–64): Wobble animation with concerned expression
  • SAD (25–44): Slow breathing with tears
  • SLEEPY (0–24): Droopy eyes with ZZZ
